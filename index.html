Personal Statement

Haiming Wang is an EDGE Architectural Technologies graduate student at SCI-Arc in Los Angeles, blending computational design, AI, and neuroscience to craft visionary, socially responsible architecture. With roots in Shanghai and a passion for merging art, science, and humanity, Haiming's work spans interactive data-scapes, generative agent-based facades, and ethical AI research that reimagines how buildings learn and respond to people and place.
